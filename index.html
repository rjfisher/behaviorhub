About Behavior Hub

What we are…

Behavior Hub offers a variety of services that have been created to make individual’s lives easier. Our inspiration came from crossing paths with people who were struggling to find solutions on how to manage challenging children. When we thought more into this reoccurring issue, we decided to create a program from scratch. The results were a uniquely, personalized program that elevates the way individuals communicate with children through collaborative problem solving.

Services

The consulting side:


The mind is our medium. We work on developing parts of the brain that link to emotional regulation. We thrive on creating unique sessions individualized for each child.

  • Individualized sessions
  • Small group sessions
  • Sessions in the home
  • Sessions in public meeting places
  • Sessions in school
  • Digital sessions

The training side:


The knowledge you have is your virtue and we’re in this to make it the greatest it can be. We design interactive, participant-led training sessions that are adaptable for any situation or environment.

  • 2-hour workshop
  • 3-week training series
  • 6-week training series
  • Workshops on the web
